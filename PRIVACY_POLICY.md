# Owl Assist Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

## Overview

Owl Assist helps users check suspicious links, files, and messages in a simple way. This privacy policy explains what Owl Assist does, what data it uses, and when information may be sent to online scanning services.

## What Owl Assist Does

- Scans files that the user chooses or shares into the app
- Checks visible links on screen when the user enables the optional accessibility feature
- Stores scan results on the device so the user can review them later
- Can perform online reputation checks when the user chooses an online scan

## What Owl Assist Does Not Do

- It does not secretly read all messages
- It does not send files or links online unless the user chooses an online scan
- It does not click buttons or send messages for the user
- It does not monitor everything in the background without user action

## Local Processing

- Local scans happen on the device
- Scan history is stored on the device
- Owl Assist does not use cloud history by default

## Online Scanning

If the user chooses an online scan:

- File hashes, links, or files may be sent to third-party scanning or threat-intelligence services
- Public scanning services may keep or share submitted samples and reports
- Owl Assist may receive scan results from its own scan service and third-party providers

Users should avoid online scanning for private or sensitive documents unless they accept that risk.

## Accessibility Feature

Owl Assist accessibility mode is optional.

- The user must enable it in Android Accessibility Settings
- It is used to inspect visible links and show assist indicators
- Passive link detection can be turned on or off in the app

## Stored Data

- Results are saved locally on the device
- Clearing history removes saved scan records from the device

## Contact

For privacy questions about Owl Assist, contact the app publisher through the app listing or support channel used for distribution.
